WC-WAVE Virtual Watershed Platform

Hello and welcome to the WC-WAVE virtual watershed platform! WC-WAVE stands for the Western Consortium on Watershed Analysis, Visualization, and Exploration.

The WC-WAVE project is funded by the National Science Foundation's Experimental Program to Stimulate Competetive Research (EPSCoR) under three grants, one for each of the partner states, Idaho (NSF Award Number: IIA-1329513), New Mexico (IIA-1301346), and Nevada (IIA-1301726). As such, Any opinions, findings, and conclusions or recommendations expressed in this material are those of the author(s) and do not necessarily reflect the views of the National Science Foundation.

For more information, please see the Official WC-WAVE Summary Page put together by the EPSCoR folks. See also westernconsortium.org for even more info.
